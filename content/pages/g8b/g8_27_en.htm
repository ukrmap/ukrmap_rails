Formation of plant cover. An additional 15 - 12 thousand years ago during the last glaciation in Europe, almost all Ukraine had been covered with grass vegetation. In the north, formed cold tundra, and South - not too warmer plains. Woods and heat-loving plants were kept only in secluded valleys in the south and in the Crimean mountains. After the ice period end antropohenovoho period formed the modern vegetation. Its species composition and distribution depended on climate change. Warm moist periods contributed to the spread of deciduous forests of beech, oak, chestnut, hornbeam, maple, and appeared in the era of cold pine-birch forests. In the southern part of the same Ukraine hundred thousand years dominated steppe vegetation.

Currently, the vegetation of our country are kinds plants that remained from the ancient geological eras. They are called relics. In Ukraine there is relict yew berry, yellow rhododendron, sword-grass marsh, marsh saxifrage and others.

On the species composition of modern vegetation and influenced Human activities that caused the extinction of some species of plants and distribution of new, imported from other areas.

Patterns of distribution vegetation. Diverse vegetation Ukraine representing more than 30 thousand species of higher plants, including Herbal prevail. Trees and shrubs only 280 types. More than 600 species higher plants are endemic - Those that meet only on this territory. Natural vegetation is preserved in Ukraine only in the forests, the meadows, swamps, slopes of hills and ravines, in nature reserves.

Plants that grow in a particular area, able to adapt to similar environmental conditions (climate, topography, geological buildings, grounds, etc.). form the plant communities. They harmoniously coexist trees, shrubs, herbaceous plants, mosses, lichens, fungi. Ukraine is most common in forest, steppe, meadow and marsh plant communities. In different parts of Ukraine, their different species composition, due differences in humidified, lighting, thermal conditions and soils. On the lower part of Ukraine vegetation cover mostly zonal - depending on latitude. In the mountains observed by high-altitude vegetation change girdles.

Deciduous forests differ in their species composition in different territories: the oak-beech common in western Ukraine oak-hornbeam - on the Right Bank, oak-maple-lime - on the Left.

Especially changing species composition of forests in mountain regions of Ukraine. In the foothills of the Carpathians distributed mainly platyphyllous forests oak, hornbeam, beech, maple, maple. On the northern foothills of Crimea distributed mainly oak forests and scrub, and southern - stunted oak and spruce forests of shrubs. The slopes of the Main Ridge of Crimean mountains covered with oak and beech forests, above are sometimes modified pine. The South Coast of Crimea appear thermophilic evergreen subtropical species: Boxwood, laurel, cypress, magnolia, sycamore, fan palm, oleander, lavrovyshnya et al. Vegetable Resources. Plants always accompany human life. First of all they provide the environment of its existence, supplying oxygen to the atmosphere. Mastering of soil nutrients and collecting solar energy with photosynthesis, plants are indispensable in human food and as animal feed. They are used as valuable medicines, building materials, raw for various industries. Plants have long served as fuel. And coal, oil, natural gas and peat are also of plant origin. Among the green world Plant man draws inspiration, aesthetic enjoyment, inspiration, refreshment and health.

Special value is Forests. They mitigate climate (in winter they freeze more easily tolerated, and in summer - Heat), reduce wind speed, longer retain moisture. The forests are hitting net well, not dry river. A distinguished scientist V. Vernadsky emphasized that the forest soil filters the effluent so well that it can not be compared water in the laboratory. The root system of trees to prevent soil erosion, landslips.

Man makes extensive use of forest wealth. Wood goes for furniture, matches, packaging, wallpaper, paper, pencils, household products, gifts. The fruits of many shrubs (rose, hawthorn, raspberry, hips, buckthorn) are used for preparing medicines. Healing properties are also many herbal plants (valerian, violet, dandelion, St. John's wort, strawberries, celandine, plantain, lily of the valley, periwinkle, etc.).. The forests are rich in mushrooms: brown, pidosychnyky, white chanterelles Oiler. They are ideal places location for sanatoriums, rest homes.

Thus, Flora provides the very man diverse resources: food, medical, industrial, agricultural (pasture and hay), recreational.

Plant protection. The importance of plants in nature and human life is essential to efficiently and intelligently use them. On Unfortunately, human impact on nature is not always positive. Destructive deforestation, crude drainage of wetlands and river flood, excessive plowing Prairie areas, destruction of natural systems and mining activities industrial wastes lead to impoverishment and destruction of plant communities, extinction of many species of plants in Ukraine. This in turn causes other negative processes. In particular, deforestation has led to a sharp reduction absorbance of soil, shallowing of rivers, drying up springs and wells. Held and changes in climate: the summer heat has increased, frequent showers rains cause floods in the rivers. And in the Ukrainian Carpathians recent uncontrolled deforestation has caused more and becoming more frequent landslides.

Irreversibility of negative processes in the plant the world has caused the need for measures to protect and restore its species and communities. Thus, in 1980, was issued Red Book of Ukraine. It describes the rare and endangered plant species that require special of their place of distribution, the measures for their preservation and reproduction. If first edition of the Red Book of Ukraine were included 150 species of plants, now them for more than 600. Among them, for example, is well known snowdrop normal, sleep-grass, primrose plain, onion bear, rhododendron east-(Red rue), alpine bilotka (edelweiss) juniper high, thousands of berries and others. Most of the endemic and rare species are Crimean Mountains and Ukrainian Carpathians.
