Seasons. We become accustomed certain rhythmic changes in the surrounding nature, which repeated from year to year. We call them seasons. Everyone is familiar natural signs of spring, summer, autumn, winter. We could easily distinguish these seasons in environment, the photos or pictures. Seasons affect our business activities, planning of working time and rest, life and health.

To organize your life and work we use these calendar, linking, say, the beginning of spring until 1 March, and summer - By 1 June. However, real changes in the nature does not coincide with calendar out of season. In astronomy seasons - A intervals between days Equinox and Solstice: Astronomical spring lasts from vernal equinox (March 21) to the summer solstice (22 June), summer - from summer solstice to the autumnal equinox (September 23), etc. But climatologists have linked the onset of the seasons go on dates averaging air temperature 0° C and 15 ° C. So spring comes a climate where the average day temperature increasing from 0 ° C to 15 ° C, summer - with temperatures over 15 ° C autumn - when the temperature decreased from 15 ° C to 0 ° C, and winter - from daily temperatures below 0 ° C.

In Ukraine, the seasons are expressed quite clearly. Springoccurs earliest on the southern coast of Crimea - the end of February, instead of northeast of the country - only in late March. For early spring characteristic unstable weather: there are sharp cooling, freezing observed in May, sometimes snow falls. The steppe zone there are dust storms. Spring begins Thunderstorms and heavy rain.

Climate resources. Solar radiation, moisture comes from rainfall, wind is an environmental resources. They belong to inexhaustible natural resources, multi-purpose. Among them distinguish energy, agro-climatic and recreation resources.

Energy climatic resources (Solar and wind) in Ukraine is quite significant and widespread throughout its territory. However, for industrial Solar electricity is expedient to use South Coast of Crimea and the steppes of the Ukraine, and wind - in the mountains areas on the coasts of seas and reservoirs and open steppe areas. Now use of energy resources in Ukraine climatic insignificant: there is a single experimental solar power in the Crimea and several small wind.

Agroclimatic Resources include warm air and soil and water supplies needed for growing crops. Important in this respect is the period of stable average daily temperature air, higher than 10 ° C, ie a period of intensive crops vegetation. Throughout in Ukraine length of this period is sufficient for growing cultural temperate plants, it is much longer in Transcarpathia and longest - of Southern Crimea, where agro-climatic Resources possible to grow subtropical culture.

Damping characteristic area coefficient moistening - The ratio of annual rainfall to vyparovuvanosti (number moisture can evaporate under these temperature conditions) over the same period. If annual rainfall is approximately equal vyparovuvanosti, the coefficient moisture is about 1. This  consider sufficient moisture. If the rate of hydration greater than 1, is excessive moisture, and if less than 1 - insufficient. In the north and west Ukraine (Polesie) Or excessive moisture adequate. There was large held areas of land reclamation. Further east cock and spreads slightly Arid and Arid Zone. Black Sea Lowland and flat Crimeaare in a very arid zone (moisture ratio less than 0.55), where for obtaining good yields of crops needed artificial irrigated lands.

Recreational climatic resources used to treat and Rest of people. Most favorable conditions are formed on the shores of seas, rivers, reservoirs and lakes in mountainous areas and forests. It combines fresh air, higher humidity, higher wind speed and the kind of regime air temperature. Recreational climatic resources are used on throughout Ukraine. For this purpose sanatoriums, rest homes, children's camps.
