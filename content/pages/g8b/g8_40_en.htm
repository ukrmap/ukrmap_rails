"Red Forest"

First blow radioactive pollution suffered a strip of pine forest (about 450 ha), which is near the Chernobyl NPP. For several days after the accident, this forest almost "Burned out" by becoming rudym. After the burial of trees in the trenches in this area were planted new ones. Now the forest is restored. However, the burial place of the dead wood become sources of radioactivity in groundwater, only exacerbated environmental problems.

 

Chernobyl zone. Around the Chernobyl NPP radius of 30 km created Exclusion Zone, Called Chernobyl zone. From She had evacuated thousands of people of 186 well-populated areas of Ukraine and Belarus. Most of the area is in Ukraine. There hiatus in 1975 settlements, and immigrants were 90 thousand people. In the zone left their homes, the graves of relatives, monuments history and culture. As a result of air emissions amounts of radioactive substances were stable and durable contamination of cesium, strontium and plutonium. These substances emit radioactive rays and are called radionuclides. They have the ability to congest in organisms, water, soil, air and long-term impact on the environment, lives of people and animals (half-lifes of cesium-137 is 24 thousand years!). Therefore, in the area of significant contamination allowed to grow crops, gather berries, mushrooms, fowl, catching fishing, drinking water, smoking wood or leaves. Studies have shown that the main number of radionuclides in the forest in the upper 10 centimeters layer soil in their needles much longer than the leaves of trees. Many pollutants accumulated in bottom sediments themselves Kiev Reservoir.

However, the lack of people and their economic activities are extremely favorable conditions for reproduction wildlife in the Chernobyl zone. Significantly increase the population of boar, elk, deer, wolves, foxes, badgers, raccoon dogs, ferrets, once again appeared almost missing moose, beaver, European lynx, seen even traces of brown bear. Populations of many species in the area are the largest in Ukraine. Unfortunately, all the animals, like plants, radioactive contaminated substances.

During the years that have elapsed since the accident, constantly working to reduce radiation contamination Ukraine. Regular monitoring of radiation levels, the influence radionuclides in various environmental components and human health. Now Chernobyl zone - a scientific ground. In 2008 there was also created to store radioactive waste from other nuclear plants in Ukraine. This area of "Radioactive" is a kind of landscape subject to the safe attitude to use such powerful energy sources like nuclear and other achievement progress.

Consequences of the Chernobyl AES will long feel the population is not only Ukraine but the world. Impact geoecological situation on health. Environmental Condition environment significantly affects the health and life expectancy. In Ukraine environmental factors along with socio-economic is determinant in fertility and mortality. Since 1992 , the mortality in our countries than in fertility rates, so the decrease of population. This appears particularly acute in regions where significant radioactive and industrial pollution (Chernigov, Kiev, Zhitomir, Donetsk, Luhansk, Dnipropetrovsk and Zaporozhye region).

Radioactive contamination in Ukraine affect human health directly and indirectly. Radiation is harmful in large doses (exposure of people, causing them death) and small (increase in the incidence of people). It is known that 95% of the radionuclides enter the body through food, especially through milk, meat, fish and forest gifts. Therefore, they should be checked for these substances, especially in polluted regions.

Agricultural products collected in the contaminated soils are often hazardous to use. Thus, in wheat Cadmium can accrue - metal, which provokes different diseases. A high nitrates, caused by excessive fertilizer can cause a general poisoning of the body. Especially dangerous to health is water pollution. With poor quality water in the body can get bacteria and viruses, harmful substances. Acting on the human body for a long time toxic substances even in small doses lower immunity (the body's resistance to diseases). Consequently, the frequent cases of different diseases, aggravated existing chronic disease.

To maintain health need a clean environment, unpolluted food and water. So when planning your business activities, providing various needs of the population, will need remember to save the environment as most urgent needs, which provides our existence.
