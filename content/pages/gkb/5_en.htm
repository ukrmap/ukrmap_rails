Ukrainian land in 14-15 centuries

 

Some historians called the Grand Duchy of Lithuania - Lithuanian-Ruthenian state. Coming the land of Russia Lithuanian princes adopted local laws, customs, language, faith. The difference was that the princes were not Russians, and Lithuanians. But the significance Kyiv has changed. With the once mighty capital of Russia, he became the center separate principality within the Lithuanian state. And in 1470 he instead principality was established Kyiv province. Princely power was transferred in inheritance, and the magistrates appointed himself Grand Duke of Lithuania.

 

 

In Fourteenth century. city again began to grow and develop rapidly. In Kiev lived for about ten thousand people. New fortifications were erected, constructed new castle, where the prince and his army. Center for Urban Life The division moved on. Here lived the majority of the city: carpenters and traders.

Situation Kyiv improved after giving it in 1499 Magdeburg law - right to self-government. Kiyani got the right to choose their managers and not obey orders from the magistrates. Their duties were to help palatine objection to the guards and building fortifications.

In 1569 the Grand Duchy of Lithuania combined the kingdom of Poland in one state Commonwealth. Kiev Voivodeship came under Polish administration. In Kiev, there are Polish officials know.

In December 1648 Bogdan victorious army of unprecedented splendor met in Kiev. Then in Khmelnytsky finally matured thought of creating your own state. Naturally, its center would become Kyiv.

Rebellion has grown into real war, which lasted even after the death of Hetman nearly half a century. As a result of this war Left Bank Ukraine and Kyiv came under the power of Russia. KIEV SOVIET (1921-1991 biennium)

Beginning Twentieth century was difficult enough for Kyiv. Saturated years to events and changed the face of the city and its inhabitants. In 1914 started The First World War, during which Kyiv was pryfrontovym city. This huge stream of soldiers, wounded, refugees. For the Russian Empire war ends revolution. After it has started even more terrible war - civil. In Ukraine many political forces, fought among themselves. The government in Kiev has changed several times a year. The city suffered damage. There was not enough food, there were problems with heating. Declining population. Someone died, someone just escaped from the horrors War. On the streets had a significant number of homeless children.

After these heady years to the events in Kiev utverdzhuyutsya Soviets. Later, Kyiv became capital of the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic within the Soviet Union - the state created by the Bolsheviks.

The first decade Soviet power can not be measured unambiguously. On the one hand increases the area Kyiv, which crosses and the left bank of the Dnieper. The number residents. Resume operation factories and plants, creating new enterprises. Implemented a program to eliminate illiteracy. Opens School professional schools and universities. Start to work great number of research institutions and design bureaus. There were literary and artistic community.

But on the other hand, new government very often rejected gained through the years. In Kiev was destroyed many historical and cultural monuments that remind of the pre-revolutionary past. Particularly affected Kiev churches. Persecution suffered many political and cultural leaders who do not support the new government.

Great suffering became the Great Patriotic War. 72-day defense of the city, the Soviet retreat and the occupation troops, fighting for the liberation of the city have brought considerable damage. Many Kiev died on the fields of war or during the occupation. It destroyed many residential homes, businesses, bridges, education and culture.
