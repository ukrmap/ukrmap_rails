Japan has an advantageous economic and geographical position at the center of the Asia-Pacific region, which has become a major economic centers of the world.

In Japan, all areas of modern developed economy. It combines high intensity of production, based on high technology and non-field, focused on high living standards. In industry, high levels of automation and robotics cybernation production, introduction of energy-saving technologies. An important feature of Japan's economy is the coexistence of a large number of small businesses with giant corporations. Six major industrial and financial groups control almost all spheres of economic life.

In the territorial structure of the country highlighted district high concentration of industry on the coast of the Pacific (Pacific industrial belt) and peripheral zone - the Sea of Japan coast. Limited resources of its own raw materials led to concentration of industrial production in areas closest to imported raw materials (east coast ports of Tokyo, Yokohama, Osaka and Kobe). It is here that formed the main industrial zone of the country, which produced 90% of industrial products. In the Pacific industrial belt holds about 70% of the population. Urban agglomeration Pacific coast (Tokyo - Yokohama, Osaka - Kobe - Kyoto, Nagoya) and located between the cities form a single system of urban settlements - Tokyo megalopolis (Tokaido), which is the largest in the world.
