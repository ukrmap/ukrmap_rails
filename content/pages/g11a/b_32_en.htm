wheat are concentrated mainly in the temperate and subtropical zones of the northern hemisphere, where sufficient heat and moisture. Cultivated is mainly winter wheat and ravine. Winter wheat yield is much higher than the spring because the spring gets a lot of moisture from melting snow and have a longer vegetation period. Winter wheat is cultivated more often than spring, but the cold snowless winter it often freeze solid, causing the need to sow in the spring under spring varieties. The largest wheat producers are represented on the map. The major grain exporters in the world market is the United States, Canada, France, Argentina, Australia, South Africa. Great potential in supplying the world market of wheat as Ukraine. Produce high-quality wheat flours, cereals, pasta and confectionery, starch, alcohol.

Rice is the main food crops for almost half of humanity. This culture of tropical and subtropical zone with long and warm summers. It is grown mostly on irrigated (flood) lands in Asia, Latin America, Africa. In most countries receive two harvests a year, so much smaller area than in wheat, rice collected almost the same amount. The biggest development came rice cultivation in Asia ( 9 / 10 of world production). Among the largest producers of rice are China, India, Indonesia, Vietnam, Bangladesh, Japan, Thailand.

Produced from corn flour, meal, starch, vegetable oil, glucose, alcohol, etc.. Widely used it as a green crop (grain and green mass). Almost half of the volume growth and exports accounted for U.S. corn. Quite a lot of corn grown in Argentina, Brazil, Mexico, China.
