The structure of the atmosphere. How can consider the structure of the atmosphere, if outside air is transparent and does not vary its different parts? We found that density varies with height, content water vapor, temperature and other characteristics of air. With these features in atmosphere distinguish separate layers: troposphere, stratosphere, the upper layers.

Troposphere - Bottom (ground) layer atmosphere. Its thickness at the poles is 9 km in high latitudes - 11 km, above the equator - to 18 km. Troposphere is the most solid layer: it is concentrated in about 80% of the total air mass. In the troposphere is nearly all water pair, from which clouds are formed and then Air. Therefore, in the troposphere formed Earth's weather.

Stratosphere placed above the troposphere and extends to a height of 55 km. The air is very thin there. It has almost no impurities and water vapor. Because clouds are usually absent. At an altitude of 20-30 km concentrated ozone gas to form ozone. It absorbs deadly ultraviolet rays of the sun, harmful to all life on Earth.
