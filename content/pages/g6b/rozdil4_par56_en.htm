The population of our planet continuously increases. In the distant past, before our era and the beginning of our own, it grew slowly. The reason is that people strongly dependent on natural conditions, it was hard to deal with epidemics and famine. The rapid growth of population began only in the last 400 years, when the dependence of human Nature has become smaller. By 1900 the population reached 1, 6 billion people. And of the twentieth century. population growth have exploded: in 1975 - 4 billion man in 1987 - 5 billion man in 1999 is 6 billion resident. The main reason for this rapid increase in population was reducing mortality due to development of technology and medicine. Even in less developed countries could overcome many diseases that took one thousand lives.

Scientists predict that in future the number of population increase.  Because humanity important target for development that meet the needs of the global population in food, education and health, environmental quality medium.

Accommodation population. With the growing population it was fast moving terrestrial ball. But people rozselyalysya unevenly. As you know, the first centers of human civilization arose in river valleys or the plains of the favorable climate - in the valleys of the Indus, the Tigris and Euphrates (Sumerian, Assyria, Babylon), Nile (Ancient Egypt), the plains of China.

Now, like many centuries ago, the natural conditions and resources determine the uneven allocation of the population on Earth. About 70% all people living in 7% of the land surface. A 15% land with harsh natural conditions - Antarctica, Greenland and Northern Islands Arctic Ocean - And are still inhabited. The average number of inhabitants per 1 km2area reflects the population density. To identify the number of population divided by land area on which it resides. So, if Earth's population is approximately 6 billionpeople and land area - 149 million km2Then population density on the planet will be 40 people per 1 km2.

Population density varies greatly by the mainland and countries. Thus, European and Asia it is almost twice the density world population, but Africa, North and South AmericaOn the contrary - less than half the world. In Asia, in areas prosperous farming population density sometimes reaches 2000 people per 1 km2, and in Europe and North America developed  industrial areas - 1 500 people 1 km2. Lowest population density - in Australia (2 people per 1 km2). In Ukraine it is 78 people per 1 km2.

Human race.Earth populated by people, different in appearance - skin color, hair eyes and face shape. These differences were so long ago, when certain groups people living far from each other. Then the different natural conditions left their impact on different groups of people. As a result of centuries of adaptation to a natural environment they formed various external signs. Since external signs of hereditary transmission from parents to children, from generation to generation, regardless of location. Groups of people with similar external signs are called human races. Scientists ethnographers are three basic human race.

European race - The most numerous. To It includes about 45% of the population. The appearance of this group race is not the same. People who live in northern areas, light emerged skin and blond hair. The people who lived in the southern region, the skin was brown, and hair dark. Representatives from major european races inhabiting way Europe, Part Asia. Many of last century migrated to other continents.
