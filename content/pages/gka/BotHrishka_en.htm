Central Botanical Garden. NBS was was established in 1935. In prewar years, there were collected a large collection of plants, but during the Nazi occupation of the plants were destroyed, and the garden has suffered considerable damage. Renaissance Botanical Garden began after the liberation of Kyiv in 1944, and to visit it was discovered only in 1964.

today by 132 acres of gardens unique collection of 12 thousand species, forms and varieties of plants from all over the world. Every spring thousands of visitors are attracted here in Ukraine, the largest collection of lilacs. In greenhouses garden presents a unique collection of tropical and subtropical plants, among which one of the largest - collection of orchids.
