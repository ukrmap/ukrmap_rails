It was the age of mass movement of tribes, which caused death of the Western Roman Empire. Migration started with the invasion of Northern Black Sea Asian tribe of Huns. Huns were forced to move to the West Goths, then bothering other Germanic tribes. Some of them joined the Huns, others were saving on new lands.
