Principal PROCEEDINGS

  • emergence of the Cossacks was caused by both internal and external factors and showed great vitality of contemporary Ukrainian.   • Zaporizhzhya Sich - Orthodox Christian-democratic republic.
  • Zaporizhzhya Sich played a critical role in the history of the Ukrainian people, especially in its national liberation struggle in the development of Ukraine's armed forces and military art, in exploration and colonization of the steppe spaces of Central and Southern Ukraine.
  • result of economic activity Cossacks was colonization of the Wild Fields (steppe between the Dniester and Don), thus expanding the living space of the Ukrainian people, which took place peacefully, without conquests foreign territories.
