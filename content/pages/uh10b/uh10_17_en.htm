The first war of Soviet Russia with the Ukrainian National Republic (December 1917-1918)

In late 1917 it appeared that Russia could not continue war. Started negotiations with the States in the Quadruple Alliance Brest-Litovsk, 2 (15) December 1917 the Russian delegation signed armistice with Germany and Austria-Hungary. Learning about  this event, the government appealed to the UPR warring states of note, where proclaiming that Petrohrad government has no right to represent negotiations the interests of all peoples of Russia, including Ukrainian. Germans realized that the presence of the Ukrainian delegation in Brest will give them additional leverage in negotiations. 13 (26) December Germany and its allies told the Central Council that they are willing to deal with UPR in the negotiation process.

Negotiations began in late December, which consisted of about 400 official and unofficial representatives of participating countries. Countries Quadruple Alliance UPR recognized sovereign state entitled to conclude  international treaties.

January 27 (February 9) in 1918 between the UPR and Germany and its allies signed a peace treaty that reaffirmed the war between contracting parties without annexations and indemnities. The boundaries between the UPR and Austria-Hungary admitted to the agreements pre-war time, and with Poland planned to identify them through further negotiations on the basis ethnographic approvals and subject to the desires of the population. Parties refused to claims for damages caused by war,  anticipated exchange of prisoners and resumption of mutual economic relations.

The signing of the Brest peace. 1918

In addition, the UPR is committed to supply Germany and Austria-Hungary 60 million pounds of bread, 2 mln 750 thousand pounds of meat and other agricultural  products and industrial raw materials. Germany and Austria-Hungary promised Ukraine to agricultural machinery, coal and other necessary raw materials and commodities.

At the time of signing of the Ukrainian delegation already knew about Bolshevik troops capture of Kyiv. She appealed to the countries Quadruple Alliance of requesting assistance against armed Ukraine expansion of the Soviet Russia. Central Council, seeking salvation for Ukraine from the Bolshevik expansion, given its under occupation Germany and Austria-Hungary. Allies agree, knowing full well that get food from Ukraine, they can only under its occupation  their troops.

German troops began to move into the territory of Ukraine 5 (18) February  Later in 1918 their support of the Austro-Hungarian army. Total number of occupying allied troops was 450 thousand people. Their offensive not met serious resistance from the Soviet troops. March 1, 1918 by Bolshevik troops left Kyiv. Again returned to the capital Central Council. Population met her restraint, because it led to  a German invaders.

The Bolsheviks tried to stop promoting the occupation forces by proclamation in the territory of Ukraine of separate regional "Republic": Donetsk-Krivoy Rog, Crimea, Odessa and others. But allied command not found them and continued the offensive. Losing power  in Ukraine, the Bolsheviks dissolved the National Secretariat and created instead of in April 1918 to guide the All-Ukrainian Bureau rebel fighting against the German and Austro-Hungarian invaders.

First command of German and Austro-Hungarian troops declared  its interference in Ukrainian affairs, trying to persuade UCR expeditious implementation of treaty obligations, but very quickly obvious weakness of the Central Council and hopelessness cooperation with it.

In early April, Chief of German forces in Ukraine issued order, which poperedzhalys Ukrainian peasants so that they do not take more landed estates, than they can shine at the same time forbade them prevent landowners sat down in the earth. When the joint cultivation of land landowners and farmers harvest was divided between them equally. Central Council regarded this order as a direct interference in the internal affairs of Ukraine, issuing a special resolution, which condemned these acts.

However, in late April, despite the position of the Central Council German command announced the formation of military courts-field over the Ukrainian citizens. Central Council protested against such a decision. Then April 26 the German command in Ukraine received Kaiser Wilhelm II of permission to change the Ukrainian government.

Complication of relations between the Central Council and allied troops took place against the backdrop of worsening political situation in Ukraine UCR rapid loss of credibility among the population and political forces. Wealthy  groups particularly irritated the leaders of socialist phraseology Central Council while the government has caused alarm declared nationalization of large landed property. They began to appeal to Command the allied troops with a request to protect them from the peasant massacres and tyranny of the Central Council. Such appeals found support in German and Austro-Hungarian authorities.

Hetman Pavlo Skoropadskyi with its environment

The basis of the Hetman's government made the moderate conservative minded officials, military and public figures. They believed that Ukrainian state should ensure the political stability that based on principles of private property. Domestic Policy Hetmanat aimed at strengthening the Ukrainian State, which had  dominate the interests of wealthy citizens. Heads the Council of Ministers was F. Lyzogub - Poltava landowner, Minister Foreign Affairs - D. Doroshenko, Minister of Education and the Arts - AM Vasilenko, Minister of Justice - M. Chubynsky.

The main objective of the Hetman's administration was the creation an effective government, the elimination of anarchy, setting economic life. Bureaucracy cited authoritarian order and repressive methods. In Ukraine, a wave of arrests Sentences, executions. Primarily for burning them, those who have recently led the local authorities carried out the confiscation of the landlords and lands. Considerable attention was paid to the creation of armed forces. Began opened special schools training officers, was deployed to form the eight buildings and several cavalry divisions. However Hetman forbidden to enter the military and political parties associations, participate in rallies and demonstrations.

One of the first acts of the new government was the restoration of the landlord tenure, which would provide state trade bread. In July Hetman in 1918 passed a law on means of combating rural ruin  economy. For his farms actually given away in available to landowners who acquired the right to use compulsory manors implements peasants. Recently started to resort to strikes field work, but the government is punishing them by imprisonment. Trying to relieve tension, Hetman promised to take steps to redemption large owners of land in empowerment for its rural poor. But further pledges did not go right.

All events took place on Ukrainian life accruing background requirements by the occupation forces against the supply of food and raw materials to Germany and Austria-Hungary, an agreement on who signed with P. Skoropadskyi Governments of the Union State. In Ukraine a real war hetman and occupation troops against the peasants. But to meet the requirements Hetman occupants failed.

Complicated was the situation in the industry that finds itself at war almost in full decline. Many businesses were destroyed, reconstruction of their missing funds. The government forced the workers for tiny fee to work intensively. A serious threat to many labor groups became close plants, including raw materials exported to Germany. In May 1918, a congress in Kiev representatives of industrialists, financiers and landowners. Under the pressure government moved in to attack workers' rights by removing the 8-hour time, limiting the freedom of trade union activities and prohibited strikes.

Thus, the burden to overcome the economic crisis has been translated into shoulders of workers. However, extreme measures have given the authorities an opportunity to change general situation for the better. Began to form national banking system innovations and currency - Hryvnia - proved very stable. It was possible to reopen the railway.

Big changes have occurred in education and a cultural area. The result this activity was the discovery of almost 150 Ukrainian schools.
