1. Netherlands under the rule of Spain in XVI century.

Netherlands (in translation - "Lower Land") known as the land located on the Lower Rhine Maas, Scheldt and the North Sea coast.

In the middle of the XVI century. Netherlands covering the territory where now are the Netherlands, Belgium, Luxembourg and part of North-Eastern France. This country had no fertile soil or minerals, suffered from floods. Its main wealth were hard-working residents. They proudly said: "God created the heavens, we - dry. 6. Formation of the United Provinces Netherlands

In response to the decision supporters of reconciliation with the Spanish King province north of Utrecht signed union, which initially five and later seven provinces united to wage war against him to complete victory. His supreme authority they proclaimed General States.

The signing of the Union of Utrecht started a new independent state - United Province of the Netherlands, most often called the Dutch republic. When Philip IIdesignated award for the murder of William of Orange, July 26, 1581 was passed an act which proclaimed complete independence of the Netherlands. This historic document was put provision that if the governor does not people are satisfied the Netherlands, people have the right to remove it. That's why people Netherlands eliminated Philippe II. In the United Provinces Netherlands left the northern provinces, and south - remained under Spain.

Summer 1584 William of Orange killed agent Philip IIBut this has not led to the collapse of the United Provinces that Spain hoped. Ruler Netherlands was elected the son of William of Orange Moritz.
