Columbus was born in Italy. Already with 14 years participated in the voyages to England and along the coast of Africa. Columbus fascinated by Italian Paolo Toskanelli views that Earth felt a bullet, and a western route from Europe to the East - the shortest. Toskanelli made a map of the world where Asia was marked on the west coast Atlantic. By his calculations, the way to China and India through Atlantic was much shorter than around Africa. However Toskanelli wrong in determining the size of Earth and, consequently, the distance from Europe to Asia. Subsequently said that it was a big mistake that led to great discovery.

 

Map and calculations Toskanelli H. Columbus convinced the ability to reach India, moving on event. With Columbus' journey had opened a new part of the world, which at first called "West-India (West Indies). The name "America" is open Columbus mainland received by traveler named Amerigo Vespucci, who studied the open land, said that it was not Asia, and the "New World". Since these lands were to mark on maps as "Amerigo Earth" and later simply "America."

Based on research Toskanelli, Columbus decided to try to reach the shores of India, sailing across the Atlantic Ocean. Council of Portuguese scientists considered Columbus proposal, acknowledged its unreal. Columbus was in despair. He went to Spain, proposed a draft of King Ferdinand and Queen Isabella. After liberation from the Arabs of Granada in 1492, which ended the period Reconquista (Spanish reconquista - reconquer) and the Arab presence in the Iberian peninsula, Spain wanted fame and power. Queen Isabella agreed with the proposal of Columbus. Having decided to organize this expedition, she, of course, risked, but its successful completion could bring many benefits for Spain. Under the contract, which signatory H. Columbus to the Spanish king and queen, in case of failure expeditions he has not received any compensation. In case of success, he had become viceroy of open land, get the title of Admiral and the tenth part of goods bought or found in public lands.

August 3, 1492 Columbus' expedition of three caravels - "Santa Maria", "Pint" and "Nino" - a garrison of 90 persons went to sea. The expedition lasted over two months. Sailors could not bear the hardship and demanded their return home. Finally, over 69 days they saw land. On the bank of a small island was installed Spanish flag and declared its possession of the Spanish king called island Holy Saviour - San Salvador. This day, October 12, 1492 is the date the discovery of America. Pursuing voyage, Columbus discovered the islands Cuba and Haiti and returned to Spain. Here he King triumphantly reported on open land to the west, which saw India but gold is not brought about. Royal court was disappointed.

As viceroy of new land, Columbus made four trips yet, during which was opened Lesser Antilles, the islands of Puerto Rico, Jamaica, Trinidad, etc., explored the coast of Central America. Opening of Columbus led to increased rivalry between Spain and Portugal for the right possess new lands. For settlement of disputes through the mediation of Pope Alexander VI, both countries signed in 1494 Tordesilyasi in agreement with limit which was held from pole to pole approximately 30-degree meridian, west of Cape Verde. All open land and water meridian west units were Spanish, to the east - Portuguese. However, this separation was done only in the Western Hemisphere. Subsequently, a new collision When opponents of Molucca Islands. Then in 1529 in Zaragoza was signed a new agreement on division in the Eastern Hemisphere in 17-degree meridian of east of the Molucca Islands. So was first in the history of colonial division of the world.

4. First round the world and Magellan its value

After death Columbus members of his expedition continued study of open land. In 1513 Spanish expedition led by Vasco Nunez de Balboa first crossed the Isthmus of Panama, looking fantastic, "Golden Country" - Eldorado. Before travelers opened the Pacific Ocean. However, they did not know its real size and named the Southern Ocean sea. After this discovery idea was revived in the Spaniards find a western route to India.

The first who found western route to India and made the first ever round the world, became Portuguese Fernando Magellan (1480—1521).

Magellan more of his life spent at sea expeditions, while serving in Portuguese king. Several times he has been traveling to Molucca islands, and perhaps even then began to think about the path of America to the islands should be reduced.

September 20, 1519 expedition of five ships went to sea and took a course on the west. Travel lasted three years. Magellan reached the southern edge of America and found Strait (she now has a name of Magellan), which is connecting two oceans. During swimming was good weather, and Magellan called the Pacific Ocean. Prolonged swimming was very difficult: the crew suffered from lack of food and water, died of scurvy and other diseases. Most of the expedition perished. Only in spring 1521 expedition reached the island off the east coast led Asia which was later named Philippine. In these islands, in a battle with local Magellan lost tribes. Two ships, which remained under the command of Sebastian d'Elkano (1487-1526), reached the Molucca Islands, and, taking a load of spices, they went home. 6 September 1522 sailors returned to Spain.
