2. Agrarian reform and daily needs of its implementation.

3. Significant concentration in Kiev scientific forces. Here were: Commission for the Study of Ancient Documents, and the Commission for description of the provinces of Kiev educational district.

Ems decree was designed in the spirit Valuev Circular, but it sharply increased the repression against the Ukrainian culture, banning: import Ukrainian-language books, printing and publishing in Russian Ukrainian original works and translations of plays for theater performances, texts to music, except with the preservation of historical monuments spelling original; community. At the same time provided material moskvofilskomu help movement in Galicia, which fought hard against the motion Ukrainian.

Separately, officials recommended Ministry of Education especially carefully chosen teachers for schools in southwestern part of the empire. Preference was given to the Russians. Same Ukrainian-teachers who were educated in Kiev, Kharkov, Odessa educational districts had to leave to work outside Ukraine.

Supervision of the Ems carried out the decree of Directorate General of censorship. Without advertising Language prosecution, officials kept that Ukrainian books not reach common people.

Ems decree was a heavy blow and for science and for the Ukrainian movement in general. It was designed to draw attention Remove Ukrainian subjects of serious cultural genres, drive Ukrainian culture stalled provincial backwardness, smash organizational centers Ukrainian movement. Ukrainian people were the only Russian Empire, in which repression suffered a national language.

Wilderness Ems decree was somewhat softened in 1881, when it was allowed to print in Ukrainian dictionaries, though the Russian alphabet. The question of Ukrainian-Performance solved since the local authorities. But in 1884, 1892 and 1895 his supplemented by new restrictions. In 1892 censor recalled inadmissibility of publications Ukrainian translations from Russian. In 1895 special circular was brought to an end printed Ukrainian chytanok children. They are also not to be printed, even if the content was quite innocent. Overall Ems decree remained valid until 1905

Ems decree had serious consequences for the Ukrainian movement, namely:

1. He limited the means by through which one could influence the awakening of national consciousness Ukrainian people.

2. Deterred the development of Ukrainian culture.

3. Has led to disruption of certain times of the Ukrainian movement in Dnieper Ukraine, but not its termination.
