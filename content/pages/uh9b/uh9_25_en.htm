M. Drahomanov

 

M. Drahomanov had far-reaching intentions. He estimated the real situation of Ukraine and compared it with the development European states. In the social movement in Ukraine due to appear Drahomanov new goal - to achieve the level of advanced European countries. He believed that Ukrainian democratic movement should combine social, economic and national principles. Patriotism and democracy, according Dragomanova derived from nature Ukrainian people, for centuries oppressed and deprived of its elite.

For the Ukrainian movement important were Dahomanova views on future politics. He advocated separation of Ukraine from Russia, stood at the Federalist positions. However, when Drahomanov this out of the opportunities to reorganize the Russian Empire. He not seen it centralized state, and the loose confederation of autonomous regions. Such views were based on his position that the Ukrainian society there were no forces that could bring the case of National Liberation Ukraine to end. Not opposing nations argued Drahomanov identity Ukrainian, their ability to solve issues of public life. Fell was deleted Drahomanov "time gone" in which he argued that under Ukrainian Russian rule more lost than gained. He made the accusation those who forgot to deal Ukrainian liberation, spilled their own heritage people abandoning the language and culture. With pain in my heart Drahomanov wrote that educated Ukrainian custom of working for everyone, not only for Ukraine and its people.

The attack on Ukrainian culture 1875-1876 biennium Drahomanov forced to leave home. In Geneva, he and other Ukrainian politemihrantamy magazine published "Community" (18781882 years), who became the first Ukrainian newspaper, where the views were summarized hromadamen and put forward a program of the Ukrainian movement. The program lay these principles as a democracy (the introduction of democratic rights and freedoms and the corresponding institutions that would protect them), federalism, kulturnytstvo, evolutionary (introduction new order through gradual reform), socialism (the introduction of social justice). Also in the program indicated that the trends of the Ukrainian movement coincide with the general direction of universal progress. However, such views Drahomanov for Kyiv community leaders were too radical. After polemics and recriminations in the 1886-1887 biennium resulted in rupture. In 1889 Drahomanov moved to Sofia, where he taught at Sophia University.

Drahomanov had influence on Ukrainian not only in Russia but also in Galicia. His ideas fell like especially young Galicia. Subsequently, there was established the first Ukrainian Socialist Party (Rus-Ukrainian Radical Party). The spread of socialist ideas and the creation of groups in Galicia bank Ukraine and helped friends Drahomanov - Nikolai Sieber and Sergey Podolynsky.

Under the influence of ideas in Drahomanov Dnieper Ukraine faced young community, which mainly hurtuvalasya students. They are attracted to the idea of combining the struggle for national and social liberation.

For the Ukrainian movement of securities were not only the ideas of Michael Drahomanov, but his sacrifice, in sequence defending their own principles. The views were Drahomanov neodyemnoyu part socio-political thought the second half of XIX - early XX century.

4. Student communities' movement 90's

90's Nineteenth century. - period of rising youth who orhanizovuvalasya in circles and larger companies with one purpose - to do something useful for our people. Unlike older workers, young branch did not want to content themselves with apolitical activities. This radical youth was called "Young Ukraine". Under this name from 1896 in Kiev there was a secret community of more than a hundred participants practiced the ideas of socialism and national independence of the Ukrainian people. According Police, similar communities was 22, and the most important ones - in Kyiv, Chernihiv Lubnah and Odessa. They were mostly students. Members of the public aware that the common goal of a united front needs to go. Based on this Ukrainian students leaders convened in 1898 First Congress student communities, which was held illegally. The Congress adopted resolution which, inter alia, proclaimed: "We, the Ukrainian Youth Group stremlyachy for political self-government, which pursued the ideals of liberty, equality, fraternity, press for equality before the law? freedom of conscience and equality of all belief, education in the native language of free compulsory guidance Science. In the political field, students seeking a constitutional freedom of political autonomy, freedom of speech, press and assembly. The resolution also put forward the requirements of mandatory students of Ukrainian cultural and political legacy, the spread of education among the people of socio-economic reforms.

A year later the Ukrainian students held a second congress, which established a Central Union. Gravamen Union programs were:

"1. Stop harmful and destructive for Our nation's young people vynarodovlennya students in Ukraine and the Russian return of all-Ukrainian students to serve the interests of the Ukrainian people.

2. Bring to the information as masses and the educated strata of society that is evil, bezvidradnyy socio-economic and general cultural condition of our nation depends on its national political slavery as a direct consequences of Russian absolutism.

The rest of the program was dedicated questions of tactics and strengthen the organization. In particular, one of the points required units members of the student community of serious study and comprehensive knowledge of all kinds spread of information on Ukrainian affairs, namely the Ukrainian literature, history, geography and ethnography. Another item required to educate students in accordance with the principles of public morality.

Programs Students' Congress reflected the enthusiasm and idealism of youth, their commitment to national ideals.

5. Brotherhood tarasivtsi

In the late nineteenth century. in Ukrainian movement among young intellectuals appeared nationalist current. First, it existed as an idea took shape later in the political unit, but not withstand competition from other streams of the national movement, again returned to the level of ideas and was represented by individuals.

It originated as a reaction to the crisis culture is movement (Ukrainophyls). It is evident that the root causes was the colonial status of the Ukrainian people in the Russian Empire.

In the early 90's Nineteenth century. Kyiv formed student group, which included MI Mikhnovskyy, SM rustle, AP Kuczynski and others. Circle maintained close connections with leading figures of the Ukrainian movement. From this group came out figures, which soon established a secret Brotherhood tarasivtsi. Even in 1891 during census movable property in Poltava province known Ukrainophile OO Rusov attracted the work of many students. The structure of group II consisted of Lipa, M. Bayzdrenko, M. Bazkevych, V. Borovik. Summer 1891r. they back the idea of loyalty oath Ukraine's liberation at the grave of Taras Shevchenko, founded a secret society, members which was later to call ourselves the Brotherhood tarasivtsi.

The first public performance of the Brotherhood was reading the speech on Shevchenko's anniversary in March 1893 in Kharkov University. That year it was published in Galician Journal of Pravda. The ideas expressed in speech, can hardly be characterized as nationalist (an independence), but on the background and apolitical kulturnytstva the general decline of Ukrainian social and political movement after Ems decree they looked truly national radicalism. In his speech, they stated the following principles:

·   Ukrainian people are separate nation;

·   as every nation, the Ukrainian national will need to work and progress;

·   full autonomy Ukraine (then this slogan no one dare not);

·   unity Ukrainian people (this slogan was the embryo of the future concept of collegiality Ukrainian State);

·   negative treatment Ukrainophyls (kulturnytstva), but recognition of its benefit to the national movement;

·   radicalism and extremism of apostates;

·   need Ukrainian schools;

·   use Ukrainian language and others.

Ideas find their fraternity supporters, but have not begun spreading through the sharply negative attitude to the other trends of the Ukrainian movement, complete neglect of social and economic issues, and also in view of the personal qualities of leadership tarasivtsi MI Mihnovskiy.

The Fellowship was established tarasivtsi the principles of underground and divided into five countries, which in the middle 90's acted in Kharkov, Odessa, Katerynoslav, Poltava, Chernihiv and some other cities.

Brotherhood members held in Basically, organizational and educational work, aimed at shaping national consciousness.

In 1893 took place in Kyiv organizational gathering, which probably had to give a slimmer organization form. But as the Brotherhood tarasivtsi had no concrete program of action and sometimes simply duplicate old kulturnytstvo in the original extreme forms (Eg, demonstrative wearing traditional dress), it was a decline late 90's virtually disintegrated. This was helped by police and prosecution of members of the organization.

7. The historic significance of community movement

Communities, despite their small number, played an important role in the formation and development of modern Ukrainian movement.

First, they completed association of two regional jets Ukrainian movement in the Russian Empire: Left Bank, which was the beginning of the revival and continued to Slobozhanschine their traditions in Cyril and Methodius Brotherhood, and of the right, rooting in the "Ukrainian school" Polish literature and extended in hlopomaniv activities. The Ukrainian movement of the Left played a leading role descendants of Cossack, on the Right Bank - the descendants of Ukrainian spolschenoyi gentry.

Second, they provide succession of generations in the Ukrainian movement - Cyril-Methodians and younger, that started activities in 50-ies

Thirdly, their Gromadovtsi fruitful research on the history, ethnography, folklore studies, statistics, linguistics have shown that Ukrainians have deep historical roots and clear ethnic characteristics defined ethnic boundaries. This action brought the Ukrainian movement need to formulate and develop a political program.

Fourth, had Gromadovtsi considerable influence on the Ukrainian movement in the Western lands.

Fifth, scientific activities hromadamen laid the foundations of Ukrainian science.
