One of the most developed heavy industry of Ukraine is metallurgy . Iron, steel, rolled, ferrous alloys, pipes - products industry. Presence in Ukraine large deposits of iron and manganese ore (under 5% and 20% of world reserves), coking coal, clay, fluxing materials caused the formation of strong metal base. Much of the material concentrated in the Dnipro and the Donbas, which together with the presence of customers (metalomistke engineering), developed transport network and resulted in significant human resources allocation in the near area businesses, Azov and Donetsk regions.

main centers of the steel industry is Dnepropetrovsk, Krivoy Rog, Dnipropetrovsk, Kharkiv, Donetsk, Mariupol, Alchevsk, Khartsizk. As seen from the map Metallurgy , and the number of hosted enterprise and the share of the region in the production of industrial products dominate Dnipropetrovsk, Donetsk, Zaporizhzhya region. Overall, the production of iron and manganese ores, iron and steel - Ukraine among world leaders. And exports of steel products for the state is one of the main sources of foreign currency earnings. The main problems the industry is pollution, the need for technical upgrading of production and quality metals.
